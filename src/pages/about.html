Attribution

Kohana reduces an organization's Mean Time To Detect (MTTD) through adversary engagement technology. This distributed deception solution is cloud native and guided by the MITRE Engage™ framework. Kohana was developed by students out of the University of California, Berkeley's School of Information in the summer of 2022. The following were involved:

  • Nate Singer
